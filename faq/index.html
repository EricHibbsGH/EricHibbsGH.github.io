Booking & pricing

  • You tell us the home size, frequency, and any add-ons; the calculator returns a flat all-in price. That’s what you pay — no upcharges on the day of, no surprise fees, no tip required.

  • Yes. The instant quote tool prices your clean and books a real time slot in under 60 seconds. We confirm with a text within minutes; no phone call required unless you want one.

  • Most homes book 3–5 days out. Same-day and next-day slots are sometimes available; the calendar in the quote tool shows live openings.

Cleaners & team

  • Every cleaner passes a nationwide criminal background check, a Georgia driving record review, and an in-home trial clean before they touch a paying client’s home.

  • On recurring plans, yes — the same lead cleaner is assigned to your home every visit. If they’re ever out, you’ll be notified before the visit and a vetted backup is sent with a copy of your home’s preferences.

  • Employees, on a W-2 with workers’ comp, payroll taxes, and benefits. We don’t use 1099 contractors. It costs us more but it’s the right way to run a cleaning business in 2026.

Day of the clean

  • Pets are welcome. Tell us in the quote tool if your pet is anxious or shouldn’t be in the same room as cleaners and we’ll plan accordingly. We use pet-safe products by default.

  • You pick a 2-hour window when booking (8–10 a.m., 10 a.m.–noon, noon–2 p.m., 2–4 p.m.). We’ll text 30 minutes before arrival.

Cancellations & rescheduling

  • Yes — reschedule any time more than 24 hours before the visit at no charge, right from the confirmation text. Inside 24 hours we charge a $30 short-notice fee to cover the cleaner’s lost shift.

  • No contracts, no cancellation fees. Email or text us before your next scheduled visit; the plan ends immediately. You only pay the recurring discount on visits already completed.

  • Skip individual visits without losing your recurring discount, as long as you don’t skip more than two in a row. After that we move you to a longer cadence to keep the price honest.

Quality & the guarantee
