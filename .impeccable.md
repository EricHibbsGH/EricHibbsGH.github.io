## Design Context

### Users
North Atlanta homeowners (Johns Creek, Alpharetta, Marietta, Buckhead, Lawrenceville, Buford, Gainesville, Canton, Dawsonville, Winder and surrounds) booking residential cleaning — plus some commercial clients. Primary context: mobile phone, often in a hurry, evaluating 2–3 providers. The job-to-be-done is "get a trustworthy quote fast and feel confident scheduling."

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, warm, professional, transparent. Three words: **dependable, welcoming, polished.** The brand should feel like a local small business that's grown up — not a slick national chain, not a scrappy one-person operation. Emotional goal: **relief** ("finally, a cleaner I don't have to vet myself").

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Palette (locked by user):** teal primary `#0D7377`, brand-red accent `#E11D29` (matches the "RED SKY" lettering in the logo), with existing dark `#1a1a2e` text and `#f0faf9`/`#f8fffe` surfaces. Do not introduce new hues — stay within this system.
- **Theme:** light mode (residential services consumed during the day, viewed on phones in bright conditions).
- **Anti-references:** glassmorphic SaaS landing pages, cyan-on-dark "tech startup" aesthetic, generic Bootstrap-style cleaning templates with stock photos of women in yellow gloves.
- **Style:** editorial-professional. Confident type, calm rhythm, meaningful whitespace. Decorative details (icons, gradients, pulses) should feel purposeful, not ornamental.

### Design Principles
1. **Trust through clarity, not decoration.** A clean hierarchy and honest pricing disclosure do more for conversion than another gradient.
2. **Mobile is the primary canvas.** Every layout decision is validated at 375px first; desktop is an enhancement.
3. **One voice across the page.** Copy is direct and specific ("Next available: Friday, Apr 17"), not marketing-speak ("Premium experiences, delivered").
4. **Motion serves comprehension.** Animations confirm state changes (toggle flipped, step advanced, form submitted) and respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. No ornamental motion.
5. **Preserve what works.** This is a revamp, not a rewrite. Copy meaning, services, pricing, and palette are fixed; typography, spacing, rhythm, and interaction details are open.

### Constraints (locked)
- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS stack (no framework introduction).
- Keep the brand palette (teal + orange) and do NOT run `/colorize`.
- Preserve all current copy meaning and all four services (Standard, Deep, Move-In, Move-Out).
