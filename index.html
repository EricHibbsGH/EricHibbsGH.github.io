100% Satisfaction Guarantee

Your happiness is our top priority. If any part of your cleaning doesn't meet your expectations, simply contact us within 24 hours and we'll send our team back to re-clean the area at absolutely no extra charge.

No fine print. No runaround. If you're not satisfied, we make it right — period.

Transparent Pricing

We believe in complete transparency. The price you're quoted is the price you pay — no hidden fees, no surprise charges, no upselling on cleaning day.

Our pricing is based on your home's size, the service type, and any optional add-ons you choose. You'll see an estimated range before you even submit your quote request.

Consistent Quality

Every Red Sky cleaner follows our detailed checklist system — the same thorough process on every visit, whether it's your first clean or your fiftieth.

We conduct regular quality audits, and our team leads inspect results to ensure nothing is missed. Your home gets the same premium treatment, every single time.

Trust & Safety

Every member of our team undergoes a comprehensive background check before their first day. We verify employment history, check references, and run criminal background screenings.

We're also fully insured and bonded, so your home and belongings are protected. You can feel completely safe and secure when our team arrives.
